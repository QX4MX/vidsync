Vidsync is an open-source project ( Gitlab ), which allows users to watch YouTube videos together by creating or joining Rooms to watch with your friends.

Create a Room

Join a Room

e.g. https://vidsync.de/room/5ed63003d51f16148ad3a4cd

Invite the Vidsync Discord Bot to your Discord Server.

The Vidsync Discord Bot will answer to every Youtube link, by creating a room for the linked video or even a whole playlist. The bot is open-source aswell ( Gitlab )
